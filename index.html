Hi, I’m Floris de Ruiter. I’m a 20-year-old Game Developer and Designer skilled in Unity C#

Quantum Shuffle

Quantum Shuffle is a first-person puzzle game where your spatial thinking is challenged. Stuck in a mysterious laboratory, you have to manipulate your surroundings to escape.

Circuit Climber

Circuit Climber is a 2D platformer game where you parkour through a desktop PC or puzzle in it's components. Hurry and escape before it overheats and you burn with it.

Drip or Drop

Drip or Drop is a game where you play as a little plant guy and try to keep all the plants hydrated while parkouring through the map. You have to try and keep the plants alive as long as possible because once 5 plants have died, it’s game over.

Pantser

Pantser is a rogue-like game where you try to survive as long as possible while natural predators are constantly trying to swarm you.

FSM AI Designs

Finite State Machine's are a mathematical model for any system that has a limited number of conditional states of being. I personally use them for AI designs.
