About Me

Hello, I'm Floris de Ruiter, a passionate game developer specializing in gameplay programming. As a third-year game development student, I have been fortunate to explore various aspects of game development and gain a good understanding of not just the programming but also running production and working with other devs and artists
While gameplay programming is my primary areas of focus, I have also delved into other parts of game development. By working on multiple released projects, I have gained valuable experience in various roles.
Whether it's being scrum leader, level design, audio integration, or UI implementation, I enjoy working in teams to bring cohesive and engaging experiences to players.
Please take a look around my portfolio, where you will find my latest projects, showcasing my abilities as a game developer. Feel free to reach out to me if you are looking for an intern or have any tips on how to improve my work.
