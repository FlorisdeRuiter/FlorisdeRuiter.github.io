Circuit Climber

Circuit Climber is a singeplayer platformer where you play as a spiderbot. Parkour through a overheating PC and enter the ethernet port to escape to the world wide web.

Platformer Controller:

For this character controller I wanted to make the player feel like they had a lot of control over the character. I did this by adding things like variable jump height, coyote time and jump buffering. Learn more.

Highscore Tracker:

While playtesting I got some feedback about adding a highscore to keep track of your best time. This ended up being a great addition since playtesters really liked speedrunning the game. Learn more.

Project Description:

For this project we had to design and develop a full game on our own.
It was also a freelance simulation where we were the producer and our teachers acted as clients.
Finally we set up a design document and contract which had to be signed by our teachers.
